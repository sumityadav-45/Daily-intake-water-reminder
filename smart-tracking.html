Smart Water Tracking

Smart Water Tracking is a core feature of AquaLife that helps users monitor, analyze, and improve their daily water intake. It records every glass of water consumed and provides insights to maintain proper hydration.

What is Smart Water Tracking?

Smart Water Tracking automatically keeps a record of how much water a user drinks throughout the day. Instead of guessing, users can clearly see their intake and compare it with their daily hydration goal.

How Smart Tracking Works

  • User sets a daily water goal (in liters or glasses)
  • Each glass of water is logged in the app
  • System updates total water intake in real time
  • Progress is stored for daily analysis

Smart Water Intake Calculation

AquaLife uses a simple and effective formula to calculate personalized water intake:

Body Weight (kg) × 35 ml

Example:
Weight = 65 kg
Daily Water Goal = 65 × 35 = 2275 ml ≈ 2.3 liters

What Data is Tracked?

  • Total water consumed per day
  • Number of glasses consumed
  • Missed intake intervals
  • Daily hydration status

Benefits of Smart Water Tracking

  • Prevents dehydration
  • Builds healthy hydration habits
  • Improves focus and energy
  • Supports digestion and metabolism
