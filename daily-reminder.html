Daily Water Intake Reminder

Daily Water Reminder helps users drink the right amount of water at the right time. Proper hydration is essential for digestion, energy, concentration, and overall health.

Why Drinking Water Is Important

The human body is made up of nearly 60–70% water. Water helps regulate body temperature, flush toxins, improve digestion, and maintain energy levels. Lack of water can cause dehydration, headache, fatigue, and poor concentration.

How Much Water Should You Drink Daily?

  • Average Adult: 2.5 – 3 liters per day
  • Hot weather / active lifestyle: 3 – 3.5 liters
  • Students / office workers: 2 – 2.5 liters

Glass calculation:
1 glass ≈ 250 ml
8–10 glasses = 2–2.5 liters
12–14 glasses = 3–3.5 liters

Smart Water Intake (Weight Based)

AquaLife uses a smart formula to calculate personalized water intake:

Body Weight (kg) × 35 ml

Example:
If weight = 60 kg
60 × 35 = 2100 ml ≈ 2.1 liters

When Should You Drink Water?

  • After waking up: 1–2 glasses
  • 30 minutes before breakfast: 1 glass
  • Mid-morning: 1 glass
  • 30 minutes before lunch: 1 glass
  • After lunch: Small amount
  • Evening (4–6 PM): 1–2 glasses
  • 30 minutes before dinner: 1 glass
  • Before sleep: Half glass

Water Reminder Interval

The best way to stay hydrated is to drink small amounts of water at regular intervals.

  • Drink water every 60–90 minutes
  • Avoid drinking too much water at once
  • Consistent intake improves hydration

Signs of Dehydration

  • Dry mouth
  • Dark yellow urine
  • Headache
  • Low energy
  • Lack of focus

AquaLife reminders help prevent these problems by ensuring regular water intake.
