Water Intake Progress View

Progress View allows users to visually monitor their daily, weekly, and monthly water intake. It helps users understand hydration patterns and identify days when water consumption was insufficient.

What is Progress View?

Progress View is a smart visualization feature that displays water intake data in an easy-to-understand format. It shows how much water a user has consumed compared to their daily hydration goal.

Daily Progress

Daily progress shows real-time water intake throughout the day. Each glass of water adds to the progress, helping users stay motivated to reach their daily goal.

  • Morning intake (6 AM – 10 AM)
  • Afternoon intake (10 AM – 4 PM)
  • Evening intake (4 PM – 8 PM)
  • Night intake (8 PM – 10 PM)

Weekly Progress

Weekly progress compares water intake for all seven days. It helps users analyze consistency and identify improvement areas.

  • Average water intake per day
  • Best hydration day
  • Lowest intake day

Monthly Progress

Monthly progress gives a long-term view of hydration habits. This data helps users build sustainable water drinking routines.

  • Total water consumed in a month
  • Hydration consistency score
  • Habit improvement trend

Hydration Status Levels

  • 0–40% → Dehydrated
  • 41–70% → Needs Improvement
  • 71–90% → Well Hydrated
  • 91–100% → Perfect Hydration

Benefits of Progress View

  • Improves self-awareness
  • Encourages consistency
  • Tracks long-term hydration habits
  • Helps achieve daily water goals
