About AquaLife

AquaLife is a smart water intake reminder system designed to help users maintain healthy hydration habits through smart tracking and reminders.

Smart Tracking

Smart Tracking allows users to record their daily water intake accurately. The system analyzes consumption patterns and helps users understand how much water they drink throughout the day.

  • Tracks water intake in real time
  • Stores daily hydration data
  • Helps identify dehydration patterns
  • Improves overall health awareness

Daily Reminder

Daily Reminder ensures users never forget to drink water by sending timely alerts throughout the day based on their hydration goals.

  • Custom reminder intervals
  • Notification-based alerts
  • Prevents dehydration
  • Encourages discipline

Progress View

Progress View visually represents daily, weekly, and monthly water intake to help users track improvement over time.

  • Graph-based visualization
  • Daily & weekly summary
  • Goal comparison
  • Motivational feedback

Healthy Habits

Healthy Habits helps users develop long-term hydration routines that positively impact energy levels, digestion, and overall wellness.

  • Builds consistent routines
  • Improves physical performance
  • Supports mental focus
  • Promotes a healthier lifestyle
